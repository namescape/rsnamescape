Frequently Asked Questions

Is buying/selling RuneScape names bannable?

Trading display names for RuneScape gold only is not against the RuneScape rules. Jagex prohibits real-world trading (RWT) for cash or out-of-game items. We never facilitate RWT or account sales.

How do I protect myself from “name snipers”?

Do not transfer a name yourself. Sniping bots monitor name releases 24/7. Only use a vetted, experienced transfer service like NameScape. See our sniper-resistant approach.

How do I know I’m trading with a real admin?

Always open a ticket inside our Discord server. Never trust unsolicited DMs—even if the profile looks identical to staff. We never DM first. Verify roles in-server and confirm ticket IDs.

What payment methods do you allow?

RuneScape gold only. No real-world money, no account sales, no out-of-game items.

Can you transfer names between Old School and RS3?

Yes—our transferrers support both OSRS and RS3. Open a ticket for step-by-step instructions and timing windows.

How do I start a safe name trade?

Join our Discord, open a ticket, and follow the instructions of a vetted admin. Keep all communication inside tickets.
