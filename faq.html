Frequently Asked Questions

Is buying/selling RuneScape names bannable?

Trading display names for RuneScape gold only is not against the RuneScape rules. Jagex prohibits real-world trading (RWT) for money or items outside the game, but gold-for-name trades are permitted. We never facilitate RWT or account sales.

How do I protect myself from “name snipers”?

Do NOT transfer a name yourself. Sniping bots and scripts scan for new name releases 24/7 and will steal your name instantly. Only use a vetted, experienced transfer service like NameScape.

How do I know I’m trading with a real admin?

Always make a ticket inside our Discord server. Never trust DMs out of nowhere, even from users pretending to be staff; scammers copy our usernames and avatars. We will never DM you first.

What payment methods do you allow?

RuneScape gold only. We do not allow real money trading, accounts, or items. Keeps everyone safe and compliant with Jagex’s rules.

Can you transfer names between Old School and RS3?

Yes, our transferrers are experienced with both game versions. Please open a ticket for specific instructions and timing.

How do I start a safe name trade?

Join our Discord, open a ticket, and follow the instructions of a vetted admin. Do not communicate outside of tickets.
