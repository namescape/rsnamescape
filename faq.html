Frequently Asked Questions

Is RuneScape name trading bannable?

No. Trading display names for RuneScape gold only is not against Jagex rules. Real-world trading (RWT) for money is bannable. Our marketplace strictly deals with in-game gold for names—never real money, accounts, or third-party currencies.

How dangerous are name snipers?

Extremely dangerous. Automated bots and human snipers are waiting 24/7 for rare name drops. If you try to transfer a name by yourself, you will almost certainly lose it. Never attempt a transfer without a trusted transferrer.

How do I safely transfer a RuneScape name?

Always use a NameScape trusted transferrer and open a ticket inside our Discord server. We use secure, time-tested methods and the safest automation to prevent snipers from stealing your name. Never attempt solo transfers or use unknown services.

Can I trust DMs from admins or staff?

No. Never trust unsolicited Discord DMs—even if they appear to be from staff. We will never DM you first. All trades and support are handled inside a Discord ticket on our server. This protects you from impersonators and scammers.

What should I do if someone offers a private deal?

Create a ticket with us. If you’re offered a deal outside of an official ticket, it’s almost always a scam. Only trade inside tickets created in our Discord. Never send gold or account info outside of our official process.

How do I get started?

Join our Discord, open a ticket, and our team will guide you step-by-step. You'll get safe, real-time help for your name trade or transfer.

What are NameScape's services?

We offer professional, secure display name transfers on RuneScape, handled by trusted and experienced staff. Our goal is to keep your names safe from snipers and scammers—no exceptions.
